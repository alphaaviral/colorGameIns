Color Game
Instructions

What the "thousands" of lines of code will do :- They will randomly generate 6 colors, which will be displayed as 6 tiles on the screen. It will choose one of those as the correct color, whose color code will be displayed on top of the screen.

What You, with your great mind have to do :- You have to guess the correct color by clicking on one of the tiles. If you choose the correct tile, all colors refresh and you get a point. But but but, if your guess is wrong, you lose a life hehe.

What in the world is the RGB code? It basically represents the ratio of Red, Green and Blue in a particular color. So for example, Pink has a code rgb(255,105,180), it will have 255 parts Red, 105 Parts Green and 180 Parts Blue. Due to more of red, the color should be reddish. And we all agree, Pink is reddish and not greenish or bluish. So, this was all about the game. Now go and Play!!

The game's aim :-To kill you by taking all your lives.

Your Aim :-To score as many points as you can, before being killed.
