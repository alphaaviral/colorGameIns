Color Game
Instructions

Heya! Here is how to play the super awesome Color Game.

What the "thousands" of lines of code will do :- They will randomly generate 6 colors, which will be displayed as 6 tiles on the screen. It will choose one of those as the correct color, whose color code will be displayed on top of the screen.

What You, with your great mind have to do :- You have to guess the correct color by clicking on one of the tiles. If you choose the correct tile, all tile get painted in that color. But but but, if your guess is wrong, the tile you clicked on disappears leaving a void in it's place. Click on the "New Game" button to start a new game after successfully deciphering the code.
